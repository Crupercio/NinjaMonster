◆ How to Play

Pokemon Loteria

Like Mexican Bingo — a caller draws Pokemon cards one at a time every 4 seconds. Mark them on your board. First to complete a pattern wins that prize. All 4 patterns can be won in the same game.

Your Board = Your Pokemon

Your Loteria board is built from Pokemon you own at Level 20 or higher. The board is a 4×4 grid of 16 Pokemon. When a Pokemon is called and it matches one of yours, your board is automatically marked. You don't tap anything — just watch it fill up.
