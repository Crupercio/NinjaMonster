◆ COMMUNITY LOOP
The Guild Album Loop
Stickers matter more when you decide where they live. Keep them for your personal album, place them permanently in your guild collection, and grow your guild level through daily contributions and weekly goals.
  • Donate stickers into a guild album and make them guild soul-bound
  • Earn guild XP from donations, rows, columns, and quest completions
  • Unlock perks like bonus Ryo from mini-games and extra board utility
  • Track progress by rarity, rows, columns, and completed generations
  • Give every member a reason to contribute each day and week

LIVE GUILD EXAMPLE

1
A member donates a Rare Pikachu sticker to the guild album.
2
The donation finishes a row and grants guild XP.
3
That row completion helps finish the weekly guild quest.
4
The guild levels up and unlocks a mini-game Ryo bonus.
Shared progress, better perks, stronger collector identity
