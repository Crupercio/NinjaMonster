The Core Mechanic
The Combo Chain System
Every move that applies a status effect can trigger a teammate whose ability matches that status. Chains can cascade up to 10 deep — turning a single well-placed move into a team-wide assault.
  • 30 unique status effects across 3 categories
  • Chains cascade automatically — up to 10 links deep
  • Anti-loop guard prevents the same pair from firing twice
  • AI opponent reads your team and counters your chain strategy
