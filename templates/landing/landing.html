◆ THE CORE MECHANIC
The Combo Chain System
Every move that applies a status effect can trigger a teammate whose ability matches that status. Chains cascade up to 10 deep — turning a single well-placed move into a team-wide assault.
  • 30 unique status effects across 3 categories
  • Chains cascade automatically — up to 10 links deep
  • 3×2 grid formation — front row acts before back row
  • Anti-loop guard prevents the same pair from firing twice
  • AI opponent reads your team (Easy / Medium / Hard)

⚡ LIVE COMBO EXAMPLE

1
Jolteon uses Thunder Wave — applies Paralysis
2
Gengar triggers on Paralysis — uses Shadow Ball
3
Espeon triggers — uses Psybeam → Confusion
4
Alakazam triggers on Confusion — Psychic finisher
